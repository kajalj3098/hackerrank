Company

We are MadOverGames LLC - a company dedicated to crafting fun and casual games for iOS and Android.

With our headquarters situated in California, we have a talented team of game creators located in New Delhi and Mohali, India who are constantly working towards creating fun social experiences for mobile.

Mission

Our mission is very clear - create fun and casual mobile games with a focus on quality.

Over the years we have created dozens of mobile games with the aim of delivering fun experiences to our players all over the world with easy to play features, engaging mechanics and adorable universes.

Passion

We live and breathe video games. The team is passionate about creating engaging fun experiences.

We believe it's our duty and responsibility to keep on delivering fun and engaging mobile games to our beloved audience, which now comprises of over a million users worldwide.
